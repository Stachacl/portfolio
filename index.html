My story:

Hello, I am Stacha, a web designer and front-end developer with the background in marketing. I am originally from Ukraine and I am permanently living in Sydney, Australia. I am a citizen of Austalia.

Since childhood I was obsessed with technology and math. Unfortunately I was discouraged from pursuing a career in IT because in early 00s in Ukraine it was not a “girl’s career path”.

My second passion has always been art and design and I have finished an art school. Then I continued my study and went to the University, where I received a bachelor degree in advertising. After managing advertising projects for 4 years I decided to switch to remote job and travel around the world.

In 2014 I started working as a freelance illustrator in IT creating designs for web and mobile apps.

While working on my IT projects I learned from my colleagues about the amazing thing called front-end development which combined the logic of programming, visual design and even user psychology in the form of collaborating with the UX discipline. I was excited! I knew this was THE place I want to be and dedicated myself to learning front end development in an attempt to change my career and achieve my lifetime dream of doing what I am truly passionate about. A job where creativity, logic, math, visual design, and psychology collide is truly a dream career path for me and I am extremely passionate to break into the field and hopefully leave my mark!

I understand that there are still a lot for me to learn, but I have proved myself as an effective self-motivated learner while completing the online web development cource and keep improving my knowledge in this field.
