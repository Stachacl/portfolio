My story:

Hello, I am Stacha, a web designer and front-end developer.

From 2009 to 2013 I was working in the Marketing industry as a project/event manager . Alongside other skills, this position taught me to be a team player and to prioritise business needs while delivering exceptional customer experience.

In 2014 I have decided to switch to a remote job. I started working as a freelance illustrator IT, creating designs for web and mobile apps. During this period of time I have learned a lot about web design, user interfaces, and user experience, as well as self-discipline and working independently.

Finally, in 2021 all my previous interests and experiences led me to the amazing thing called front-end development . I was excited to start learning to code. I know this is THE place I want to be, and I am truly passionate about it. A job where creativity, logic, math, visual design, and user experience collide is a dream career path for me. I am extremely passionate to break into the field and hopefully leaving my mark!

I understand that there is still a lot for me to learn, but I have proved myself as an effective self-motivated learner while completing the online web development course and keep improving my knowledge in this field.
